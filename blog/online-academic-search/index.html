How results are processed

  • Parallel search — all 7 sources queried simultaneously, not sequentially
  • Automatic deduplication — same article from multiple databases appears only once
  • Combined scoring — relevance to query + citation count + abstract presence
  • Unpaywall enrichment — free PDF links added for articles without URLs
  • Top 60 results — most relevant only, no noise
